Accommodation

Experience the ultimate convenience and comfort during your visit to the NIT Nagpur Tech Fest. Our accommodation facilities offer a range of options to suit your needs, whether you're a participant, speaker, or guest. From cozy single rooms to spacious dormitories, we have accommodations to accommodate individuals and groups of all sizes.

Each room is equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay, including high-speed internet access, air conditioning, and 24-hour security. Our friendly staff is available round the clock to assist you with any queries or requests you may have during your stay.

Charges: Our accommodation rates are affordable, starting from 500 Rs per night for single occupancy. Group discounts are available for larger bookings.

Instructions: Guests are requested to adhere to the accommodation rules and regulations to ensure a pleasant experience for all. Quiet hours are observed after 10 PM, and smoking is strictly prohibited indoors. Pets are not allowed in the accommodation premises.

Note: Please note that accommodation availability is limited and bookings are on a first-come, first-served basis. We recommend booking your accommodation in advance to secure your spot.

Note: Prohibited items include pets, smoking indoors, and loud noises after 10 PM. We strive to maintain a peaceful environment for all guests and appreciate your cooperation in adhering to these guidelines.
